Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 23, 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Dreams are deeply personal. We take your privacy seriously and will never sell your dream data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Introduction

DreamTrue ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered dream interpretation service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username (via Replit Auth/Google SSO)
  • Dream Content: Text or voice recordings of your dreams
  • Context Information: Optional life events, emotions, stress levels you choose to provide
  • Payment Information: Processed and stored by Stripe (we never see your full card details)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (features used, frequency, session duration)
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system, IP address
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage patterns to improve our service
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and functionality

2.3 AI Processing Data

  • Your dream content is sent to Anthropic (Claude AI) for interpretation
  • Anthropic's data retention policy: 30 days for abuse monitoring, then deleted
  • We do not use your dreams to train AI models

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Generate AI dream interpretations, save your dream journal
  • Improve Quality: Analyze patterns to improve interpretation accuracy
  • Communication: Send service updates, trial conversion emails, support responses
  • Billing: Process subscription payments via Stripe
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms
  • Research: Aggregated, anonymized analysis (never individual dreams)

4. How We Share Your Information

We will NEVER sell your dream data to third parties.

We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): AI interpretation processing (30-day retention, then deleted)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Replit: Hosting and infrastructure
  • Email Service: ConvertKit/Mailchimp for transactional and marketing emails
  • Analytics: Plausible or Google Analytics (anonymized)

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law:

  • To comply with legal process (subpoena, court order)
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale (with 30 days notice)

4.3 With Your Consent

We may share anonymized dream content if you explicitly opt-in to:

  • Community sharing features (coming soon)
  • Research studies (aggregated data only)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: HTTPS/TLS for data in transit, PostgreSQL encryption at rest
  • Authentication: OAuth 2.0 via Replit Auth, session management with secure tokens
  • Access Control: Limited employee access, principle of least privilege
  • Monitoring: Regular security audits and intrusion detection
  • Backups: Daily encrypted backups retained for 30 days

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing for direct marketing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know whether we sell or disclose your information (we don't sell)
  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Opt-out of sales (not applicable - we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

6.3 All Users

Regardless of location, you can:

  • Export Your Data: Download your dream journal as PDF (Premium feature)
  • Delete Your Account: Request deletion via email to support@dreamtrue.app
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from emails at any time
  • Manage Cookies: Adjust your browser settings

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained as long as your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 30 days, then permanently deleted
  • Legal Obligations: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal requirements
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely

8. Children's Privacy

DreamTrue is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Users aged 13-17 should have parental consent. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Parents: Contact us at support@dreamtrue.app if you believe your child has provided information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

By using DreamTrue, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries outside your residence, which may have different data protection laws.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses for EU data).
