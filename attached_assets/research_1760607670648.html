How It Works
1. Symbol Extraction

AI identifies key symbols, emotions, and actions from your dream description.

2. Multi-Layer Analysis

Simultaneous analysis across scientific, psychological, and cultural frameworks.

3. Evidence Weighting

Results weighted by scientific validation level and empirical evidence strength.

4. Confidence Scoring

Each interpretation receives a confidence score based on source agreement.

5. Source Transparency

Clear attribution to research sources and methodology used.

Quality Assurance
  • Expert Validation: Licensed therapists review interpretations
  • Bias Detection: Automated systems detect cultural and personal biases
  • Cultural Sensitivity: Multi-cultural perspectives included
  • Continuous Learning: System improves with user feedback
  • Privacy Protection: All dream data encrypted and secure
Limitations

Dream interpretation is not an exact science. Our AI provides evidence-based insights but should not replace professional psychological or medical advice. Always consult qualified professionals for serious concerns.
